üìä Analytics & Privacy Information

Wulkan Kibo uses analytics tools to improve user experience and understand how visitors interact with our content. We take your privacy seriously and only collect analytics data with your consent.

Google Analytics What We Track

When you consent to analytics, we use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymized data about:

  • Page Views: Which recipes and pages are most popular
  • User Behavior: How visitors navigate through the site (e.g., from homepage to recipe pages)
  • Traffic Sources: Where visitors come from (search engines, social media, direct visits)
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and screen size (for responsive design improvements)
  • Geographic Data: Country and city-level location (not precise location)
  • Engagement Metrics: Time spent on pages, scroll depth, video views

Vercel Analytics Performance Monitoring

We also use Vercel Web Analytics for privacy-friendly performance insights:

  • Page Load Times: How quickly pages load for visitors
  • Core Web Vitals: Performance metrics (LCP, FID, CLS) to ensure fast, smooth experiences
  • Traffic Patterns: Peak usage times and popular content
  • No Personal Data: Vercel Analytics doesn't use cookies and doesn't track personal information

üîí Why This Matters

Analytics help us:

  • Create Better Content: Understand which recipes are most loved so we can create more like them
  • Improve Site Performance: Identify slow pages and optimize loading times
  • Fix Issues: Detect broken links or navigation problems
  • Optimize for Mobile: Ensure great experience across all devices
  • Plan Future Content: Know what African cuisine topics interest you most
